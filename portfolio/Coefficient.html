Coefficient

Coefficient is a data consultancy offering data science, engineering, machine learning and other AI-related services as well as bespoke training courses.

Working closely with the founder, I designed and developed the brand identity. Used moodboards and interviews to help tease out the essance of what makes coefficient unique and display what it can offer.

Process
Discovery

Researching the competition found the kinds of aesthetic associated with very technical conputer usage. Whereas, in interviews with the Founder, I learned the specific appeal of coefficient was for the inexperienced. The coaches are mmotivated by the desire to make machine learing more approachable.

Development

Using mood boards and interviews, it became clear to me that the USP is the friendlyness factor. Coefficient should make complicated skills feel approachable. So I leaned into the visually describing algorithms using things found in nature.

The colors and shapes too were to be soft and inviting

Design

The logo alludes to describing data via the overlapping of graphs in a soft and flowing shape. Like a venn diagram, the relationship between coefficient and their client is colaborative. The brand name helps to carry this across.

Electric blues and greens are often associated with technology and data science, but have an air of unpenetrating difficulty.

The type kept lower case to emphasize the humbleness. The italics are helpful as the f's are reminicent of a mathmatical intergral symbol.

The approachable and fun tone extended to the website content, using a toy robot to describe machine learning.

Product

I built the site on squarespace, to allow easy editing post-contract.
