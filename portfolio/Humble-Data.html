Client: Humble Data
Role: Branding
Platform: Web
Humble Data run workshops for those who aspire to work in data science and the tech industry, with focus on increasing inclusivity and encouraging a culture of mentorship.

Working closely with the founders, I designed and developed the brand identity; how the business is described visually, it's name and logo.

Research & Development

Rather than try to compete, I looked for similar projects to identify visual iconography which the target audience will be able to interpret as a product aimed towards them. We didnt have a name, which does most of the work in describing the product.
Data analysis of user feedback survey

As there are many tech initiatives, it was a battle between targeting a specific demographic and maintaining a sense of welcomeness to all who'd approach.

Brand Design

I looked for similar projects to identify visual iconography which the target audience will be able to interpret as a product aimed towards them. Many many many conversations with the team. Made moodboards.

Humble Data's logo visually describes a varied set in python, describing diversity in a group. Cutout rough and ready is reminicent of counter culture movements, while also describing the practice as imperfect (there is room for error)
