Hi, nice to meet you! I'm Sam Ballard - an interactive digital designer, working in a variety of disciplines, since 2012.
What I do

I've been making things as long as I can remember. I can not stop, and have never shied away from learning a new craft. From animation to UX, if it's interactive, I make it. I work well as a fingers-in-all-the-pies design lead, in all forms that could take.

I spend most of my time designing games and ethical services for small companies, start-ups and charities. If you have a project you're passionate about, I want to help you make it happen.
