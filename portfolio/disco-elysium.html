Client: ZA/UM
Role: UI • UX
Platform: Nintendo Switch
Disco Elysium is an open world role playing game, where you're a detective with a unique skillset and a whole city to carve your path across.

As a part of a job interview for ZA/UM, I was tasked with re-developing the Disco Elysium UI/UX to reformat the game for the Nintendo Switch.

Taking into consideration controls and Switch trends, I first desected the game's existing design, and rebuilt using parts of the existing design elements; codifying the interactions into a more consistent design language.
