About


Hello! I'm Sam Ballard, one person with many creative ambitions.
Sunlight After Dark is a collection of all the things I've made.

Based in London, UK. Previously worked with Punchdrunk, The Wikimedia Foundation, NHSx, Restoration Games, Channel 4, HTC, UEFA, BMW, and many many many more. I work in a variety of styles, but if you check on my portfolio, you'll get an idea of my flavor.
